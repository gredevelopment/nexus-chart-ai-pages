Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how NexusChartAI collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect any personal data from users.
  • Pictures taken by you in the app are uploaded to our AI agents for processing. These images are stored securely in a cloud service so you can view them later in the History page of the app.

How We Use Your Pictures

  • Your pictures are used only to provide AI-powered features and to allow you to preview them in your History page.
  • We do not use your pictures for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

  • Your pictures are sent to our AI models for analysis to provide app features. We do not sell or share your pictures or any information with other third parties, except as required by law.

Data Security

  • We use reasonable measures to protect your pictures stored in our cloud service.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@gredev.io.
